My Research

"To decompose is to create" by incorporating Nature in Design - a Research through Design(RtD) I participated this year.

As a research assistant, I applied the notion of decomposition on ceramic-making to explore the creative process of reconfiguration through deconstruction, achieved different unexpected effects from naturally materials.

It is through this project, I began to understand that in many cases, one does not simply create a final product. Instead, it is through designing and adjusting, we combine the uncertainty with nature, and that eventually leads to the final product.

My mentor had pubulished the paper "Decompostion as Design".
